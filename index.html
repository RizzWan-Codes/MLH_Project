How Territory Run Works

Four simple steps to turn your daily movement into an exciting competition

01

Start at Safe Zone

Every player begins with a small protected area. This is your home base where your trail begins and must return.

02

Move & Create Trail

Walk or run outside your safe zone. Your GPS tracks every step, creating a visible trail on the map in real-time.

03

Close the Loop

Return to your safe zone to complete the loop. The enclosed area becomes your captured territory permanently.

04

Earn & Compete

Gain coins and climb the leaderboard. Capture more area than other players to dominate your server!

Watch Out for Competitors!

If another player crosses your open trail before you close the loop, your run fails and you capture nothing. Move strategically and stay alert to protect your path.
